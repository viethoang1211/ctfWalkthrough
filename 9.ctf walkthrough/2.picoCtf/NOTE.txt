Any hints about entering a command into the Terminal (such as the next one),
will start with a '$'... everything after the dollar sign will be typed 
(or copy and pasted) into your Terminal.

Takes challenge 147 as example
https://play.picoctf.org/practice/challenge/147

$ wget https://mercury.picoctf.net/static/2d24d50b4ebed90c704575627f1f57b2/flag
